1. Introduction

1.1 Porpuse

This is a website designed for the lovers of nature, who like to camp and want to visit many places. Here they can find a list of campgrounds where they can reserve a place to camp as many days as they want,even give feedback about a certain place.

1.2 Product Scope

The scope of this website is to provide users a list of safe places to camp ,which contain reviews from real users . Every camp place is verified by a admin before it's posted ,to prevent fake locations or bad conditions.Our goal is to have a comunity of trusting people who can help other people who want to live the same experience and go in the same places.

2. Overall Description

2.1 Product Functions

The main functionalities of this website are:

  • Create account for users
  • Management for accountD
  • Posibility of registered users to add a camp siteD
  • Users can add a review with rating and photos or videosR
  • Users can reserve a campgroundR
  • Every camp has informations about price, location, contanct , utilities and weather.
